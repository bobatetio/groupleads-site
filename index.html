What you can do with Group Leads

We give everything you need to automate lead collection and sell more of your products

Facebook Group Lead Generation

Eliminate manual data entry. Group Leads automates member sync to your CRM on autopilot, saving you time and money. Focus on your business while we handle the growth. Fully compatible with the latest Facebook layout.

Facebook Group Automatic Approval

Stop manual approvals. Group Leads auto-approves members based on your specific criteria while instantly syncing their data to your CRM and Google Sheets. Streamline your onboarding and ensure only quality leads join your group. This feature is optional.

Auto Message Declined Group Members

Improve lead quality by messaging members who skip onboarding questions. Send automated, custom messages based on your criteria to reclaim potential leads and gather deeper audience insights. Keep your group professional and spam-free while maximizing every membership request.

Auto Decline New Group Members

Identify and block bot-driven spam automatically. Simply input common bot responses into Group Leads to auto-decline suspicious accounts before they join. Protect your community’s integrity and eliminate manual moderation with our proactive anti-spam shield.

Send Welcome Message To New Group Members

Automatically send personalized welcome messages to new members. Use custom tags like [first_name] or [last_name] to ensure every interaction feels natural and professional. Maintain high-value engagement while keeping your workflow on autopilot.

Message New Group Member Before Approval

Message applicants who haven't completed their onboarding while their requests are still pending. Reclaim potential leads and gather essential market research before taking any action. This powerful function is now available directly within the Group Leads Chrome extension.

Apply Tag To New Members (via CHATSILO)

With Group Leads you can now automatically tag your new members with tags from Chatsilo Chrome extension.
